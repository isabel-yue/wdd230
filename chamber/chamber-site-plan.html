Boise Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Purpose

"The Boise Chamber of Commerce website is your go-to resource for supporting local businesses, connecting with the community, and accessing valuable resources, all aimed at fostering economic growth in Boise, Idaho."

Scenarios

Scenario 1: Local Business Owner

"As a local business owner in Boise, how can I become a member of the Chamber of Commerce, and what benefits and resources will I gain from membership?"

Scenario 2: New Resident or Visitor

"I'm new to Boise. Can you provide information about the area, including popular attractions, local dining options, and upcoming community events?"

Scenario 3: Event Organizer

"I'm planning a business event in Boise. How can the Chamber of Commerce assist me in promoting and organizing my event, and are there any networking opportunities available for participants?"

Our Membership benefits

1. NP Membership (Non-Profit Organizations)

Cost: Free


This membership level is designed exclusively for non-profit organizations. Enjoy a range of benefits to support your mission, including:

  • Access to exclusive resources
  • Networking opportunities with other non-profits
  • Participation in community events
  • Listing in our non-profit directory

2. Bronze Membership

Cost: $50 per year


Our Bronze Membership is perfect for organizations looking for added support and opportunities:

  • Access to all NP Membership benefits
  • Priority registration for events
  • Discounted rates on special training sessions
  • Promotion in our newsletter

3. Silver Membership

Cost: $100 per year


Upgrade to our Silver Membership for enhanced benefits:

  • All Bronze Membership benefits
  • Increased exposure on our website
  • Access to exclusive workshops
  • Discounted rates on advertising opportunities

4. Gold Membership

Cost: $200 per year


Elevate your organization's presence with our Gold level and enjoy premium benefits:

  • All Silver Membership benefits
  • Premium listing in our non-profit directory
  • VIP access to all events and training
  • Priority placement on our website
